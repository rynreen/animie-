Adventure

Pokemon Top 1

Ash Ketchum dreams of becoming a Pokemon Master. With a love for Pokemon and adventure, the ten-year-old becomes the sole trainer of a Pikachu that becomes his best friend. Together, their journeys around the world involve meeting new people, new Pokemon, and new discoveries.

A Place Further Than the Universe Top 2

When a girl meets a quiet classmate with over 50,000 yen in her possession, it becomes their moment to shine as each other’s supporters. A group of young high school girls embark on a journey to Alaska. Pain, comfort, love, and letting go all come together in this anime where you realize you don’t have to be alone.

Sword Art Online Top 3

A popular fantasy game becomes deadly when players literally become their avatars. As that popular movie phrase goes: You die in the game, you die for real. But one player might be able to turn the tables and gain a real-life love interest.

One Piece Top 4

Luffy dreams of becoming the King of the Pirates. With his Strawhat crew in tow, he ventures the ocean in search of its treasure. Along the way, the world slowly begins to learn his name.

Naruto Top 5

At 12 years old, Naruto Uzumaki finds out that he possesses the Nine-Tails fox, a creature that once terrorized the village when he was only a baby. Often ridiculed and disrespected by even those in his own team, the student ninja vows to become the village’s future Hokage. But first he has to get his head out of the ramen bowl enough to pay attention!

Fairy Tail Top 6

A world filled with mages and wizards, dragon slayer wizard Natsu hopes to find his long-lost father. Along the way, he meets bubbly-yet-dimwitted Lucy, and other characters to fit their awkward family mold. They take down criminals, discover secrets, and always take a moment for a snack.

Jojo's Bizarre Adventure Top 7

Bizarre doesn’t even begin to describe the Joestar family. In a series where each member gets their moment to shine in every episode, rivalries and witty banter ensue. And often or not you’ll be more distracted by the brilliant (and very handsome) animation to even pay attention to the plot.

That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime Top 8

After being killed, a man is brought to a different time and reincarnated as a slime (no joke). Accepting his new life, he comes across other characters who are in dire need of his help. And despite looking like a transparent putty, he makes it clear that he is this world’s greatest hero.

Luping the Third Top 9

Lupin the Third is a master thief aided by his group of crafty characters. An expert shooter, a samurai, and a female fatale, Lupin’s antics are well-known around the world. Capturing him has become Inspector Zenigata’s life goal.

Fullmetal Alchemist Top 10

After their mother dies due to illness, brothers Edward and Alphonse try to bring her back with the art of alchemy. It backfires, resulting in Edward losing both an arm and a leg to save his brother from being taken, whose soul is now placed into a suit of armor. They begin the journey of State Alchemists in order to restore their bodies.
